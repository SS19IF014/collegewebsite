Welcome To Government Polytechnic Mumbai

Government Polytechnic Mumbai is an academically autonomous institute of Government of Maharashtra established in 1960, one of the oldest and prestigious institute in Mumbai region and in the state of Maharashtra. Institute got academic autonomy in the year 1994.

Institute Had undertaken various Projects like World Bank Assisted Project, Canada India Industry Institute Linkage Project, Technical Education Quality Improvement Program Phase II, etc. Institute has very good liaison with industries.

Institute is offering 3 years of AICTE approved diploma sandwich pattern programme

Gym

Government Polytechnic Mumbai have facility off Gymkhana is the regulatory body governing all sports, cultural and extra-curricular activities at GPM. Gymkhana plays pivotal role in overall development of students. It gives platform students to showcase their talent in sports and cultural event. Gymkhana activities help to improve skills like dedication, involvement, balance and leadership. Students of all departments take active participation in various activities conducted through Gymkhana. Gymkhana provides facilities for major games like volleyball, kabaddi, kho-kho, cricket at the institute playground. Facilities for indoor games like carrom and chess are made available to the students.
